FAQ

Got Questions?

?
Everything you need to know before you go.

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is ready — reach out any time.

Contact Us →
01 What are the park's opening hours?
Skylark Adventure Park is open daily from 10am to 10pm. On Fridays and Saturdays we stay open until midnight. Seasonal events may have extended hours.
02 Is parking included with admission?
Parking is $15 per vehicle, available at the main entrance at Sky Plaza. Premium front-row spots are $25. Ride-share drop-off is free at Gate B.
03 Can I bring food and drinks into the park?
Outside food and non-alcoholic drinks in sealed containers are welcome. We also have dozens of dining options across all five zones if you'd rather fuel up on site.
04 Are there height requirements for rides?
Yes — thrill rides have minimums for safety. Skybreaker (48"), Lightning Drop (52"), Comet Racers (46"), Cloud Swing (42"). Zephyr Glide and all Cloud Harbor family rides are open to all ages with no restriction.
05 Do tickets expire? Can I use them another day?
Tickets are valid for the date selected at purchase. To reschedule, contact us at least 24 hours in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate. Fast Pass Add-ons are date-linked and cannot be transferred separately.
06 Is Skylark accessible for guests with disabilities?
Absolutely. Skylark is fully ADA-compliant with accessible pathways, rest areas, and ride accommodations throughout the park. Accessibility Passes are available at the info desk in Sky Plaza.
