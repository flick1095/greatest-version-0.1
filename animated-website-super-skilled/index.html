FAQ

Got Questions?

What are the park's opening hours?
Skylark Adventure Park is open daily from 10am to 10pm. On Fridays and Saturdays we stay open until midnight. Seasonal events may have extended hours.
Is parking included with admission?
Parking is $15 per vehicle and is available adjacent to the main entrance at Sky Plaza. Premium front-row spots are $25. Ride-share drop-off is free and available at Gate B.
Can I bring food and drinks into the park?
Outside food and non-alcoholic drinks in sealed containers are welcome. We also have dozens of dining and snack options throughout the park if you'd rather fuel up on site.
Are there height requirements for rides?
Yes — our thrill rides have minimum height requirements for safety. Skybreaker (48"), Lightning Drop (52"), Comet Racers (46"), Cloud Swing (42"). Zephyr Glide and all Cloud Harbor family rides are open to all ages.
Do tickets expire? Can I use them another day?
Standard tickets are valid for the date selected at purchase. If you'd like to reschedule, contact us at least 24 hours before your visit and we'll do our best to move it. Fast Pass Add-ons are linked to the ticket date and cannot be transferred separately.
Is Skylark accessible for guests with disabilities?
Absolutely. Skylark is fully ADA-compliant with accessible pathways, rest areas, and ride accommodations. We offer Accessibility Passes at the info desk in Sky Plaza for guests who need additional support.
